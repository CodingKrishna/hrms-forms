Instructions to Candidate: Please complete the questionnaire in its entirety. Dominion Energy requires all candidates to complete a Health Evaluation Questionnaire (HEQ). The purpose of the HEQ is to assist the Corporate Medical Director (CMD) evaluate the medical history and determine if there are medical conditions or prescription medications prescribed that could affect a person's ability to perform their duties safely and effectively. You may be contacted to provide more information or to request a statement from your physician if the CMD has any concerns after reviewing your medical history.
